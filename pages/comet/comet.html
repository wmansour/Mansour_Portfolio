About

This group of talented souls was forged together by the mighty gods and goddesses to make YOU laugh. They seem merely but a team of hard working improvisers making group piecess and two person scenes for your enjoyment, but do not write them off as such. Their skills and abilities go beyond what you may assume at first glance. It is their bond and friendship that creates the power they hold and that for which so many fight. However, it is only this group of worthy folk that have been endowed such power.
